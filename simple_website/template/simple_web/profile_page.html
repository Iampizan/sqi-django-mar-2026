Ogunkolati Oluwatofunmi is a brilliant but unconventional cybersecurity analyst who works by day for a major tech firm and by night investigates digital crimes that slip through the cracks. Known for her sharp intuition and calm demeanor, she has a talent for seeing patterns others miss. Raised in a bustling city, Oluwatofunmi developed her love for technology at a young age, teaching herself programming from borrowed library books. Despite her serious profession, she has a playful sense of humor and a deep loyalty to her close circle of friends.
